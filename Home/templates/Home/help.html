About Us

What is Health Net?

Health Net is a web accessible portal that allows for patients, doctors, and nurses
to interact in one system that can be accessed from anywhere with internet connection.
Giving the patients more control and oversight into their health care by allowing
access 24/7 to their medical information associated with their account. 

Features 

-Easy and immediate sign up
-24/7 access to your health information
-Ability to schedule appointments through your calendar
-HIPPA level security of your personal information
-Online exporting of your medical information 
-Never miss an appointment with our messaging/email system
